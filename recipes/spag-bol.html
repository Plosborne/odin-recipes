Spagetti Bolognese

Description

This homemade spagetti bolognese recipe has been in the Osborne family for decades. It transcends flavour, scinitillates the tastebuds and empowers even the dullest of pallets.

Ingredients

  • 500g Beef mince
  • 400ml Passata
  • 200g Spagetti
  • 1tbsp Basil
  • 1tbsp Oregano
  • 15ml Soy sauce
  • 30ml Tomato paste
  • 16g Beef stock
  • 2 Garlic cloves
  • 1 Carrot
  • 1 Onion
  • 10ml Olive oil
  • A pinch of pepper

Steps

  1. Finely chop the onions and carrot
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan at medium/high heat
  3. Once hot, add the onion and carrot and cook for 5 minutes
  4. Add mince to the pan, breaking it up with a wooden spoon, and cook for 5 minutes
  5. Meanwhile, add the spagetti to another saucepan, top with boiling water and cook for 12 minutes
  6. Combine the beef stock with 75ml of boiling water and the soy sauce
  7. Finely chop or grate the garlic
  8. Add the beef stock mix, garlic, passata, bail, oregano and tomato paste to the mince
  9. Leave to simmer for 10 minutes
  10. Once the spagetti is cooked, drain it and plate it up
  11. Serve the bolognese on top of the spagetti and top with cheese(optional)
