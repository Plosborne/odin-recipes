Spagetti Bolognese

Description

This homemade spagetti bolognese recipe has been in the Osborne family for decades. It transcends flavour, scinitillates the tastebuds and empowers even the dullest of pallets.

Ingredients

  • 500g Beef mince
  • 400ml Passata
  • 200g Spagetti
  • 1tbsp Basil
  • 1tbsp Oregano
  • 15ml Soy sauce
  • 30ml Tomato paste
  • 16g Beef stock
  • 2 Garlic cloves
  • 1 Carrot
  • 1 Onion
  • A pinch of pepper
